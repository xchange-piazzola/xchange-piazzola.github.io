raz

"I like Bologna and Piazzola as a market, I have this job, which is more like being in a fair: a lot of people pass by, I prefer staying in Bologna, in other towns there is not as many people.”

Thirsty-six years old, Raz has been selling in Bologna for "many, many years". Orginially from India, he has always sold 'oriental' and Middle-eastern products, including fabrics, tobacco, hookahs, and clothes. His stand is filled with color and a pleasant smell from his smoking products.

Raz loves Piazzola, yet always prefers the spring season in the market: in the winter the snow deters customers and also makes the day job tougher.
