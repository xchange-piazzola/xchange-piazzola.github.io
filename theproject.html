The Project

As a mediator between seemingly innocuous recordings—visual and auditory—of daily exchanges, xChange also serves an educational purpose to the viewer in relation to themself and the reality of the society surrounding them

Welcome to xChange, the virtual exhibition on Bologna’s Piazzola. Turn your audio on and feel free to wander around the website as you wish. Our purpose is that of researching, documenting, and contextualizing the interactions and exchanges taking place in Piazzola from 2019 on. By situating the market and its exchanges in the past and in the present, xChange provides a space for identification, social integration and cultural production. The virtual exhibition works like a time-capsule that everyone can contribute to with their own material and immaterial experiences.
