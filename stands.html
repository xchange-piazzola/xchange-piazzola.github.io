Welcome to the stand exhibition -- a documentation of some of the stories, sights, and sounds found in the market in 2019. Take a look at some stories and the links between them.

Here you will find the stories of five singular characters: Billy, John, Mrs. Munir, Raz, and Mery. Feel free to begin with whichever one you like, and to wander through their stands as you desire. You will be able to learn more about the variety of people that spend their Fridays and Saturdays selling at Piazzola, how they came to be there and about their products.
